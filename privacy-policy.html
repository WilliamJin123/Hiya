Hiya — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Hiya ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by William Jin. This policy explains what data the app handles, why, and the choices you have. Questions: jinwilliam.jin@gmail.com.

Data we collect and store

When you use Hiya, the following is stored in our database (hosted by our processor, Supabase) and tied to your account so it can sync across your devices:

  • Account details — your email address and a display name, if you create an account. (You can also use the app with an anonymous account that has no email.)
  • An account identifier — a random ID used to associate your data with you.
  • Content you create — the names or labels you give the people you meet, your notes about conversations, self-improvement notes, how an interaction felt, the place names you attach, and the dates and times you log.

Handled on your device only (not collected)

  • Approximate location — if you grant permission, Hiya uses your approximate location only to suggest nearby place names while you log where you met someone. This location is used on your device to bias search suggestions and is never stored or transmitted to us. Only the place name you choose to save is stored.
  • Reminders — the daily reminder is a local notification scheduled on your device. We do not operate a push server and collect no data to send it.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide and operate the app: to save your logs, show your history and insights, and sync across your devices. We do not use it for advertising, we do not sell or share it, and we do not track you across other apps or websites. Hiya contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Who can access it

Your data is protected by row-level access controls so that only your account can read or modify it, and it is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Our database and authentication are provided by Supabase, which processes and stores data on our behalf. See Supabase's privacy policy.

Retention and deletion

We keep your data until you delete it. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from inside the app: Settings → Delete account. This is immediate and cannot be undone.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data (for example under GDPR or the CCPA). You can delete everything yourself via in-app account deletion, or contact us at jinwilliam.jin@gmail.com to exercise any of these rights.

Children

Hiya is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA), and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
